PeacockAway – Smart Field Protector

Humane, Solar-Powered Solution to Prevent Peacocks From Damaging Crops

About Project Components Needed Advantages for Farmers

About the Project

In many farming regions, peacocks enter crop fields during night and cause serious damage by eating young plants and trampling soil. Farmers lose both yield and time in guarding the fields. The PeacockAway system uses motion-activated sensors, flashing lights and sound, combined with solar power, to gently but effectively deter peacocks without harm. It’s safe for livestock and humans, affordable for small farms, and easy to install.

Components Needed

  • Arduino UNO R3 board
  • PIR Motion Sensor (mounted at ~70 cm height)
  • Solar Panel (10 W or more) + Rechargeable Battery
  • Motion-activated controller (Arduino code)
  • High-power LED or strobe light unit
  • Sound module + Speaker (plays randomized alarm or predator calls)
  • Mounting pole and weather-proof enclosure
  • Reflective tapes and spinning panels for passive deterrence
  • Manual ON/OFF switch for farmer access

Advantages for Farmers

With PeacockAway:

  • Crop damage from peacocks is significantly reduced.
  • No lethal methods required — ethically and legally compliant.
  • Less labour required for night-watching or guarding fields.
  • Solar power means minimal running cost once installed.
  • Simple components make maintenance easy for rural settings.
  • Improved yield and better crop survival = higher incomes for farmers.
